About Us
Wilnes Management LTD is a Vancouver-based North America market entry and business growth partner helping Chinese brands and manufacturers expand into Canada and the United States.
We support companies through strategic market entry, business development, and local execution to enable sustainable growth in North America.
 
What We Do
Market Entry Strategy
We help companies define their positioning, understand the North American market landscape, and build effective go-to-market strategies.
Business Development
We connect brands and manufacturers with distributors, retailers, and OEM/ODM partners across Canada and the United States.
Growth Execution
We support channel development, partnership management, and on-the-ground execution in the North American market.
 
Who We Work With
Chinese consumer brands entering North America
Manufacturers seeking OEM/ODM opportunities
Companies expanding into new distribution and retail channels
Businesses requiring a North America local representative
 
Why Work With Us
We are a Vancouver-based North America market entry and execution partner with real on-the-ground capability.
We combine deep understanding of Chinese manufacturing and brand ecosystems with practical experience in the North American retail and distribution landscape.
Our bilingual and bicultural team reduces communication friction, improves execution efficiency, and accelerates cross-border decision-making.
We don’t just advise — we help execute real market entry, partnership development, and business growth in Canada and the United States.
