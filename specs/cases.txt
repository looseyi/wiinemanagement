
案例一
协助中国护肤品牌 90 天内跑通北美市场“0到1”冷启动


核心里程碑 (Key Metrics)：
启动周期：90天完成市场进入准备
合规落地：Health Canada & FDA 双认证支持
商务进展：成功推进 5 家本土主力零售/分销商谈
一、客户背景
某国产中高端功效护肤品牌（拥有8个核心SKU），在没有海外团队、也没有国际市场运营经验的情况下，希望进入加拿大及美国市场。
该品牌在国内具备一定市场基础，但在北美市场尚无品牌认知、渠道资源及销售网络。

二、项目挑战
	•	北美市场品牌认知度为零
	•	缺乏现有分销商及零售渠道资源
	•	产品配方需符合加拿大 Health Canada 化妆品法规
	•	团队英语商务沟通能力有限，缺乏海外运营经验

三、我们的解决方案
	•	协助完成加拿大 Health Canada 及美国 FDA 化妆品合规支持
	•	北美市场调研及竞品分析与定位策略制定
	•	搭建Shopify英文独立站并完成基础本地化优化
	•	制定Instagram与TikTok社媒市场进入策略
	•	执行KOL/KOC种草投放（200份样品）
	•	对接加拿大零售买手及区域分销渠道

四、执行过程
	•	完成加拿大及美国市场合规资料准备与备案支持
	•	搭建并上线Shopify独立站，完成本地化品牌呈现
	•	执行200份红人样品投放计划
	•	启动3个零售渠道及2个分销商的商务沟通
	•	收集北美首批真实用户反馈

	•	项目成果
           跑通北美市场“合规-冷启动-渠道拓展”全链路闭环
成功搭建北美本土零售及分销商准入管线
通过精准红人置换，获取首批核心目标市场的真实用户好评与测品反馈。
项目正式进入商业合作与渠道谈判阶段





🟦 案例二
加拿大精品买手店 · 自有品牌供应链开发

一、客户背景
温哥华某精品美妆买手店计划开发自有品牌产品线，但缺乏中国供应链资源及跨境采购经验。

二、项目挑战
	•	缺乏可靠的中国化妆品代工厂资源
	•	缺乏系统化供应商筛选与评估体系
	•	缺乏跨境采购及质量控制经验
	•	缺乏产品开发流程管理能力

三、我们的解决方案
	•	在华东地区筛选并对接化妆品代工厂资源
	•	根据产品需求及预算筛选3家合格工厂
	•	协调样品开发及跨境物流交付
	•	制作英文供应商能力介绍资料（Capability Deck）
	•	提供MOQ、价格及质量标准等商务谈判支持

四、执行过程
	•	在中国筛选并评估3家符合要求的化妆品代工厂
	•	协调样品打样及跨境寄送全流程
	•	负责客户与工厂之间的中英双语沟通对接
	•	整理工厂能力资料（产能、资质、生产体系等），用于决策判断
	•	协助搭建初步商务对接与报价沟通框架


	•	五，项目成果
	•	成功筛选出3家符合条件的代工厂并进入候选名单
	•	完成样品开发并顺利寄送至加拿大客户
	•	协助客户建立起一套标准化的海外供应商评估与长效筛选机制。
	•	项目已顺利过渡至产品合规测试与本地化市场适配性评估阶段




🟦 案例三
北美商务代表服务 · 中国护肤品牌

一、客户背景
某中国功能性护肤品牌已完成产品合规备案，但缺乏海外团队支持，需要北美本地商务代表协助渠道拓展及日常商务沟通。

二、项目挑战
	•	缺乏海外业务拓展人员
	•	无法直接对接加拿大零售商及分销渠道
	•	缺乏本地关系维护及沟通能力
	•	缺乏持续商务跟进机制

三、我们的解决方案
	•	作为北美商务代表提供持续执行支持
	•	提供中英文商务沟通及邮件支持
	•	对接加拿大零售商及分销渠道
	•	协助样品寄送及跟进管理
	•	支持红人合作沟通及执行协调

	•	执行过程
	•	与加拿大3家零售渠道建立初步业务沟通并进入对接阶段
	•	完成样品寄送及全流程物流跟进，确保顺利交付至目标客户
	•	持续维护渠道沟通节奏，收集并整理阶段性反馈
	•	协助推进红人合作沟通与执行落地跟进


五、项目成果
	•	成功建立3个零售渠道沟通关系
	•	所有样品均已成功交付至目标渠道方
	•	建立基础渠道沟通与执行流程
	•	形成持续跟进的商务关系网络


🟦 Case Study 1
90-Day North America GTM Launch for a Chinese Skincare Brand

Key Metrics:
Time-to-Market: 90 Days
Compliance: Health Canada & U.S. FDA cleared
BD Pipeline: Initiated negotiations with 5 major regional retailers/distributors

Background
A mid-to-high-end Chinese skincare brand with 8 SKUs sought to enter the Canadian and U.S. markets without any overseas team or prior international operating experience.
While the brand had strong domestic performance, it had no presence, distribution, or awareness in North America.

Challenge
Zero brand awareness in North American market
No existing distributor or retail network
Product formulation required compliance with Health Canada cosmetic regulations
Limited English communication capability within internal team

Our Strategy

Health Canada + U.S. FDA cosmetic regulatory compliance support
North American market research and competitive positioning strategy
Shopify-based English e-commerce store development
Instagram & TikTok go-to-market strategy
Influencer seeding campaign (200 KOL/KOC samples)
Initial outreach to Canadian retail buyers and regional distributors

Implementation
Completed regulatory compliance documentation for Canada and the U.S.
Built and launched Shopify storefront with localized positioning
Executed influencer seeding campaign (200 samples distributed)
Initiated discussions with 3 retail channels and 2 distributors
Collected first wave of real consumer feedback from North American users

Results 
Successfully completed initial North America market entry phase
Established an initial retail and distributor pipeline
Generated real consumer feedback from target market
Moved into commercial negotiation phase


Current Status
Ongoing — Compliance completed, store launched, influencer campaign executed, currently in distributor negotiation stage.



🟦 Case Study 2
Private Label Supply Chain Setup for a Vancouver Beauty Buyer

Background
A boutique beauty buyer store based in Vancouver aimed to develop its own private label product line but lacked access to reliable Chinese manufacturers and cross-border sourcing experience.

Challenge
No direct access to verified cosmetic manufacturers in China
Lack of structured supplier evaluation system
No cross-border sourcing or quality control experience
No established product development workflow

Our Strategy
Sourcing and screening cosmetic manufacturers in East China
Shortlisting 3 qualified factories based on product requirements and budget
Coordinating sample development and international logistics
Developing English capability decks for supplier communication
Supporting commercial negotiation (MOQ, pricing, QC standards)

Implementation
Identified and evaluated 3 manufacturing partners in China
Coordinated sampling and cross-border delivery process
Facilitated bilingual communication between buyer and factories
Structured supplier capability documentation for decision-making
Supported initial commercial negotiation framework

Results
3 manufacturers successfully shortlisted
Sample development completed and shipped to Canada
Structured supplier evaluation process established
Product testing phase initiated by client

Current Status
Testing phase — Samples delivered, client evaluating product-market fit and quality performance.


🟦 Case Study 3
North America Retail & Distributor Development (Retainer Model)

Background
A Chinese functional skincare brand with completed regulatory compliance required a North America-based business representative to support channel communication, retail outreach, and ongoing partner management.
The brand had no overseas team or local execution capability.

Challenge
No dedicated overseas business development personnel
No direct communication with Canadian retailers or distributors
Lack of local relationship management capability
No structured follow-up system for partnerships

Our Strategy
Acting as the North America-based business representative
Bilingual business communication and email coordination
Retail and distributor outreach in Canada
Sample coordination and logistics follow-up
Influencer collaboration communication and management

Implementation
Established communication with 3 Canadian retail channels
Coordinated sample delivery and tracking process
Managed ongoing partner communication workflow
Supported influencer collaboration coordination and feedback loop

Results
3 retail channel discussions initiated
Sample delivery completed successfully
Structured communication system established between brand and North American partners
Active pipeline of retail and distributor relationships created

Current Status
Ongoing — Providing continuous North America business representation and channel management support.
